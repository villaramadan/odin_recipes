Sinigang na Baboy

Description

Pork soup in sour broth with okra, kangkong, eggplant, and string beans. This dish is best during the cold weather.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 1 bunch spinach/kangkong
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 12 pieces string beans (cut in 2in length)
  • 2 pieces tomato (quartered)
  • 3 pieces chili
  • 2 quarts water
  • 1 piece onion (sliced)
  • 2 pieces taro (gabi; quartered)
  • 1 pack sinigang mix (good for 2L water)

Steps

  1. Heat the pot and put in the cooking oil.
  2. Saute the onion until its layers separate from each other.
  3. Add the pork belly and cook until outer part turns light brown.
  4. Put in the fish sauce and mix with the ingredients.
  5. Pour the water and bring to a boil.
  6. Add the taro and tomatoes then simmer for 40 minutes or until pork is tender.
  7. Add the string beans (and other vegetables if there are any) and simmer for 5 to 8 minutes.
  8. Put in the spinach, turn off the heat, and cover the pot. Let the spinach cook using the remaining heat in the pot.
  9. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
